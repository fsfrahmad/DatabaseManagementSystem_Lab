Q10
SELECT E1.FirstName + ' ' + E1.LastName as EmployeeName, DATEDIFF(YEAR, E1.BirthDate, GETDATE()) AS Age, DATEDIFF(YEAR, E2.BirthDate, GETDATE()) AS [Manager Age] FROM Employees E1 JOIN Employees E2 ON E1.ReportsTo = E2.EmployeeID AND E1.BirthDate < E2.BirthDate;
***


Q11
